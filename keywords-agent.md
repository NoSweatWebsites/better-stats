A new agent to be added along the side of the menu called Keywords Research

What this agent is
A decision engine for organic growth
Combines human-level strategy with real search data
Replaces traditional SEO tools with clear actions, not dashboards
What it takes in
Niche / industry
Target audience
Business goal (traffic, leads, sales)
Optional: website or existing content
What it does (end-to-end)
Defines the core opportunity in your market
Identifies a primary keyword to anchor strategy
Breaks the space into clear topic clusters (6 Circles method)
Expands into real search demand (keyword data)
Analyses what’s actually ranking on Google
Identifies weaknesses in competitor content
Scores opportunities based on:
demand
difficulty
intent
business value
How it thinks
Starts with strategy first, not data
Uses data to validate and refine decisions
Prioritises what will actually move the business
Cuts low-value ideas instead of listing everything
What it outputs
Primary growth opportunity
Keyword clusters grouped by intent
Top priority keywords to target
Quick wins (easy to rank opportunities)
Content gaps competitors haven’t covered
Recommended pages to create (with slugs)
Content pillars and structure
Execution roadmap (what to do, in order)
Short-term sprint plan (first 2 weeks)
Longer-term plan (30 / 60 / 90 days)
What makes it different
Doesn’t just give keywords → gives decisions
Doesn’t rely on tool “difficulty scores” → uses real SERP analysis
Doesn’t optimise for traffic alone → focuses on business impact
Doesn’t overwhelm → forces prioritisation

keywords agent flow = 

User fills multi step form like our current onboarding form
   ↓
App calls Google Ads API
   ↓
App gets keyword data
   ↓
App fetches SERPs
   ↓
App scrapes ranking pages
   ↓
App sends structured data + SKILL.md instructions to Claude
   ↓
Claude produces strategy JSON / markdown
   ↓

   ↓
App exports PDF
